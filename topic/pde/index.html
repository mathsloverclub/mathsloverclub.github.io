Solve the following simultaneous DEs:
  1. \( \frac{dx}{x^2} = \frac{dy}{y^2} = \frac{du}{nxy} \)
  2. \( \frac{dx}{mu - ny} = \frac{dy}{nx - lu} = \frac{du}{ly - mx} \)
  3. \( \frac{dx}{x^2 - yu} = \frac{dy}{y^2 - ux} = \frac{du}{u^2 - xy} \)
  4. \( \frac{dx}{u(x+y)} = \frac{dy}{u(x-y)} = \frac{du}{x^2 + y^2} \)
  5. \( \frac{dx}{x(y^2-u^2)} = \frac{dy}{y(u^2-x^2)} = \frac{du}{u(x^2-y^2)} \)
  6. \( \frac{dx}{x^2-y^2-u^2} = \frac{dy}{2xy} = \frac{du}{2xu} \)
